Programs

From the classroom
to the community.

AGC's programs connect students and educators directly with the people and communities most affected by the global issues they study — transforming abstract challenges into deeply human ones.

Rather than learning about human rights or climate justice only through textbooks, participants engage directly with community members and explore how global challenges are lived and responded to in everyday life.

Community members are approached as individuals with knowledge, resilience, creativity, and important perspectives to share. Our goal is learning rooted in listening, reflection, humility, and genuine human connection.

Educator retreats

Tembi Village, Yogyakarta — Yabbiekayu Eco-Bungalows

Field-based retreats and professional learning experiences for teachers. Educators explore new pedagogical approaches, learn alongside community partners in Java, develop curriculum materials, and bring deeper, more grounded perspectives back to their classrooms. Based at Yabbiekayu — an eco-friendly resort and social enterprise surrounded by rice fields in Tembi Village.

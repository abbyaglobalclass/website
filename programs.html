Programs

From the Classroom to the Community.

AGC programs connect students and educators directly with the people and communities most affected by the global issues they study — transforming abstract challenges into deeply human ones.

Rooted in place-based learning, our programs take place in Yogyakarta, Java, Indonesia — in partnership with local communities, artists, farmers, and educators.

Our approach

Learning Rooted in Listening.

Rather than learning about human rights or climate justice only through textbooks, participants engage directly with community members, visit local projects and organizations, and explore how global challenges are experienced and responded to in everyday life.

Community members are approached not as passive hosts, but as individuals and communities with knowledge, resilience, creativity, and important perspectives to share. Our goal is learning rooted in listening, reflection, humility, and genuine human connection.

In partnership with

All AGC programs are delivered in partnership with Via Via Travel — an organization committed to responsible tourism, cultural exchange, and community-based travel in Yogyakarta, Java.

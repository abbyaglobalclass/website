Our approach
"Educators learn alongside communities — not just from them."

AGC educator retreats are designed around the same principles as our student programs. Community members participate as teachers and knowledge holders, not simply as hosts.

Educators engage with local histories, livelihoods, cultural traditions, and environmental practices — building authentic relationships and deep contextual understanding that transforms how they teach.

Home base

Yabbiekayu Eco-Bungalows

Located in Tembi Village, south of Yogyakarta, Yabbiekayu is an eco-friendly resort and social enterprise surrounded by rice fields and rooted in sustainable design principles.

It provides a space for reflection, collaboration, and immersive learning connected to local communities and environmental sustainability — the perfect base for educator retreats.

In partnership with

All AGC retreats are organized in partnership with Via Via Travel — committed to responsible tourism, cultural exchange, and community-based travel in Yogyakarta.

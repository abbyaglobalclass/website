About us

Justice-centered education,
rooted in community.

A Global Classroom develops justice-centered curriculum for the classroom and immersive learning experiences rooted in human rights and sustainable development — from New York to Indonesia and beyond.

What drives us

Inquiry-driven learning

Source-based resources exploring global politics, climate justice, inequality, peace and conflict, and human rights through real-world case studies and diverse perspectives.

Community partnership

We partner with communities to design field studies and place-based educational experiences that connect students directly to the people most affected by the issues they study.

Global to local

We work with teachers, schools, and organizations to create meaningful experiences that connect classrooms to the world — grounded in respect, reciprocity, and justice.

Justice-centered pedagogy

Every resource and experience centers justice — asking students to engage critically with power, inequality, and the lived experiences of communities around the world.
